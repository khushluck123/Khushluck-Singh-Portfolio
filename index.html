Learning Journey

Learning Timeline

2023

In 2023, I was 13 years old in Delhi, staring at my first Python script. I had no mentor, no expensive bootcamp, and no idea where to begin. The tutorials I found were either too shallow to be useful or too expensive to afford.

Building My Path

So I built my own path. I started building — small scripts, then automation tools, then machine learning projects. Every project taught me more than any course ever could. But I noticed something: most Indian students faced the same wall I did. Quality tech education was locked behind paywalls and jargon.

Why NeuralSyntax Exists

That's why NeuralSyntax exists. It's not a portfolio — it's a mission. A YouTube channel, an open learning initiative, and a growing library of tools designed to make AI and programming education free, practical, and accessible for every Indian student who wants to build. The goal isn't to collect certificates. It's to build technology that helps the next generation of students learn smarter, build faster, and think bigger.
